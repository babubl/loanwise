Advisor Insight

From a long-term wealth perspective, reducing high-interest debt like home loans provides a guaranteed, risk-free return equivalent to your loan’s interest rate.

Strategic prepayments made early in the loan lifecycle have a disproportionate impact on total interest saved. Switching banks should be considered only if the net savings clearly exceed the switching costs within a short period.
