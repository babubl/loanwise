Learn: How Home Loans Really Work

Why interest feels high in the early years

In the initial years of a home loan, most of your EMI goes towards interest because the principal outstanding is very high.

Why small prepayments matter

Even small extra payments early in the loan reduce principal, which compounds into large interest savings over time.

EMI reduction vs tenure reduction

Reducing tenure saves more interest overall. Reducing EMI improves monthly cash flow. Loanwise helps you choose based on your situation.

When bank switching helps

Switching banks helps only if the rate difference is large enough and the switching cost is recovered early.
